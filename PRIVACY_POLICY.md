# Privacy Policy for FastNotion

**Last Updated:** January 30, 2026

## Introduction

FastNotion ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you use FastNotion, we may collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: When you log in through Manus OAuth, we collect your user ID, name, and email address.
- **Notion Integration Data**: When you connect your Notion account, we store your Notion OAuth access token securely to enable the app's functionality.

### Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app:

- **App Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the app, including the databases you select and the frequency of task creation.
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, and app version.
- **Log Data**: Error logs and diagnostic information to improve app performance.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Service Delivery**: To provide and maintain the FastNotion service, including creating tasks in your Notion databases.
- **Authentication**: To verify your identity and manage your account through Manus OAuth.
- **Notion Integration**: To access your Notion workspace and perform actions on your behalf (only with your explicit permission).
- **Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the app's functionality and user experience.
- **Support**: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- **User Data**: Your account information and Notion access tokens are stored securely in our encrypted database.
- **Local Storage**: Some preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms.

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
- **Secure Token Storage**: Notion OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely.
- **Access Controls**: We implement strict access controls to limit who can access your data.

## Third-Party Services

FastNotion integrates with the following third-party services:

### Notion

- **Purpose**: To create and manage tasks in your Notion databases.
- **Data Shared**: We share only the information necessary to perform the requested actions (task title, properties, etc.).
- **Privacy Policy**: [Notion Privacy Policy](https://www.notion.so/Privacy-Policy-3468d120cf614d4c9014c09f6adc9091 )

### Manus Platform

- **Purpose**: To provide authentication and hosting services.
- **Data Shared**: User account information (ID, name, email).
- **Privacy Policy**: [Manus Privacy Policy](https://manus.im/privacy )

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access**: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction**: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
- **Deletion**: You can request deletion of your account and associated data.
- **Revocation**: You can revoke Notion access at any time through the app settings or Notion's integration settings.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

## Children's Privacy

FastNotion is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
